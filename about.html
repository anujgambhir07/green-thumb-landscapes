Who we are

Started with a spade and a love of green.

Green Thumb Landscapes began eight years ago with one belief: that everyone deserves an outdoor space they actually enjoy. What started as weekend garden makeovers has grown into a full design-build-maintain team — but we've kept the same hands-on, personal approach.

We're proud to be locally owned and operated. When you call us, you talk to the people who'll be in your garden — not a call centre. That's how we like it, and our clients seem to agree.
